Integrated Coating Services : Redefining Excellence in Surface preparation & corrosion management

Integrated Coating Services Limited is a leading provider of high-quality coating solutions, abrasives, and maintenance equipment for marine and industrial applications. We supply protective and marine coatings, blasting abrasives, spray painting equipment, and safety materials designed to meet the demanding needs of onboard and dry-dock operations. Built on a foundation of innovation, reliability, and technical expertise, we are dedicated to delivering durable and efficient solutions that meet international standards. Our team works closely with clients to ensure every product enhances performance, extends asset life, and upholds safety across all environments. Through consistent service delivery, strong partnerships, and a customer first approach, Integrated Coating Services Limited continues to build a reputation for excellence providing trusted solutions that protect, preserve, and perform.

our story

Integrated Coating Services Limited has established itself as a trusted name in surface protection, delivering dependable coating solutions, blasting equipment, and abrasives for marine and industrial applications. With a steadfast focus on innovation, safety, and quality, we empower industries to achieve greater efficiency, durability, and performance. Guided by visionary leadership and a commitment to excellence, we continuously embrace advanced technologies and uphold a service-driven approach that prioritizes customer satisfaction. Every product and solution we offer reflects our dedication to raising industry standards and supporting the long-term success of our clients across every project.
